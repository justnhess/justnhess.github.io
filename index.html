Building on the Web: New Skills, New Tools, New Opportunities.

I feel a great sense of accomplishment in building something from the ground up. My dad was a carpenter. In my younger days, I spent countless hours helping him with all sorts of building projects. I always admired the way he (without any formal blueprints) could construct a building from a stack of lumber, boxes of nails, bundles of shingles, and an assortment of tools. While I didn't inherit Dad's carpentry skills, I loved the idea of creating a website for the world to see with nothing more than my ideas, a computer, a few pictures, and an assortment of programming tools.

Eventually, this site is going to serve as my online resume and portfolio. As my web programming skills improve, I hope to add new features and more functionality to the site.
