Highlights of my past, present, and future career pursuits.

I feel a great sense of accomplishment in building something from the ground up. My dad was a carpenter. In my younger days, I spent countless hours helping him with all sorts of building projects. I always admired the way he could take a stack of lumber, a box of nails, some bundles of shingles, and an assortment of tools and turn it into a building. While I didn't inherit Dad's carpentry skills, I love the idea of being able to sit down at a computer with my thoughts, some pictures, some lines of code, and an assortment of programming tools and build something of value.

Eventually, this site is going to serve as my online resume and portfolio. As my web programming skills improve, I hope to add new features and more functionality to the site.
